We're opening a limited number of **Founders Rate Scale Plan** spots for Idaho service businesses.

Normally our Scale plan — a conversion-focused website, Operations Hub CRM, and a 24/7 AI employee — is $600/month all-in. For the first ten Founders, it's **$100/month for the first year** (plus usage-based costs for phone minutes, texts, and AI time).

After the first 5 spots are claimed, the rate jumps to $200/month. After all 10 are gone, it goes to full price.

## Why this exists

It's not about cheap pricing. It's about case studies.

We want to work closely with a small group of serious Idaho service businesses and build **real** case studies, testimonials, and before/after stories with partners who are all-in. The discount is our way of compensating you for being public about the results.

## Who this is for

Strong fit if you:

- Already run a legitimate Idaho service business with an EIN.
- Have some leads and customers coming in today.
- Want to seriously improve your online presence and follow-up systems.
- Are comfortable being featured as a success story.

Not a fit if you:

- Are a startup still testing whether the business works.
- Want "set it and forget it" with no input from you.
- Are shopping purely on the lowest possible cost.

## What's included

**Website** — A custom 5-page site: home, services, about, social proof, contact. Built to convert, hosted and maintained by us.

**Operations Hub** — A white-labeled CRM that centralizes lead capture, follow-ups, and booking across forms, calls, and texts. Replaces 5+ disconnected tools.

**AI Employee** — A 24/7 digital assistant that answers calls, collects info, books appointments, and handles messages within the rules we set together.

## How it works

1. **Apply** — submit your business details and rough lead volume.
2. **Fit call** — we talk through your needs and make sure we can actually help.
3. **Onboarding** — we build, configure, and train everything together.
4. **Launch & tune** — real calls and leads start flowing; we refine as we go.

## The fine print

- Limited to a small number of spots.
- $100/month applies only to year one.
- Phone minutes, texts, and AI usage are billed separately at standard rates.
- It's an application — we pick based on fit, not first-come-first-served.
- After 12 months you can transition to standard pricing (with loyalty considerations) or exit cleanly.
