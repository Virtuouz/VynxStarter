## Private, gated, and on the water

Through a private gate and down a long arc of black locust trees, the main residence sits at the edge of a spring-fed pond, with the Boise River running along the eastern property line. Deeded river frontage is rare in this corridor; this parcel has 168 feet of it.

## Main residence

Single-level design with soaring pitched ceilings in the great room, a wood-burning Rumford fireplace, and a chef's kitchen opening to a covered loggia. The primary wing is sited for privacy with a spa bath, dual dressing rooms, and a private walled garden.

## Guest house & amenities

A separate 1,280-square-foot guest cottage has its own kitchen and laundry. A gunite pool with adjoining cabana, an outdoor kitchen under a cedar pergola, a regulation pickleball court, and a heated four-bay garage complete the grounds. Whole-property generator, Starlink redundant connection, and an automated irrigation well.

## The setting

Eagle's River District offices, schools, and the Downtown core are six minutes by car. The Greenbelt trail connects directly from the eastern easement.
