## A rare west-facing Loffs Bay address

Level walk-out lots on Lake Coeur d'Alene's south shore change hands perhaps twice a decade. This one has 103 feet of rip-rapped shoreline, a permitted boat lift and jet-ski dock, and unobstructed sunset exposure across the bay toward Tubbs Hill.

## The residence

Built in 2003 by a well-known Spokane builder and thoughtfully updated in 2022, the shingle-style residence opens to the water through a great room wrapped in divided-light casements and anchored by a basalt fireplace. The chef's kitchen has a second prep kitchen, scullery, and a glass-walled wine room for roughly 600 bottles.

Five bedrooms include a primary suite with its own lakeview terrace, and a lower-level bunkroom that sleeps eight with an adjacent game room and media lounge.

## Dock & grounds

A full-length Trex dock steps down to a covered boat slip with lift and two personal-watercraft ports. The terraced lawn was rebuilt in 2021 with new irrigation, native plantings, and a fire-wise defensible perimeter. A detached three-bay carriage house includes a gym and guest studio above.
