## A quiet side of McCall

Tucked into the pines on the east side of Payette Lake Road, this timber frame was raised in 2008 by a well-regarded Boise builder using Douglas fir from central Oregon. The frame is exposed throughout — twelve-inch posts and beams pegged with oak, a hand-forged iron stair, and a massive fieldstone fireplace that anchors the great room.

## The main house

Three bedrooms up, a primary on the main, and a loft office overlooking the living room. Radiant slab heat throughout. A summer screened porch was added in 2020 off the kitchen — it's where every breakfast ends up being eaten from late May through the first snow.

## Outbuildings

A finished sauna cabin sits at the edge of the lot beside a plunge pool. A detached two-bay garage has a guest studio above with its own three-quarter bath. Deeded access to a shared dock on Payette Lake is approximately 400 yards down a forest path.

Brundage Mountain is a twelve-minute drive; Ponderosa State Park and the McCall summer trail network begin at the property line.
