## Honest scale, done well

Not every great Boise home needs to be three thousand square feet. This 1908 Craftsman is a complete, well-proportioned house on a quiet North End block — three bedrooms, two baths, and none of it wasted. Original knotty fir floors, all of the built-ins intact, and a primary suite addition done in 2021 that is so thoughtful you'll miss where the old house ends and the new one starts.

## Garden

The rear garden was featured in Sunset in 2022. A dwarf orchard, a gravel courtyard with a cast-iron cafe table, and a small shingled studio at the back that's currently used as a pottery studio and could be a home office, guest bedroom, or the best kind of reading room.

## Location

Two blocks from Hyde Park, three from Camel's Back, and a 12-minute walk to Downtown. Walk Score 78. A quiet block without being a boring one.
